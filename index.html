Design Summer helps interns interested in design meet each other, exchange ideas, and build community. We believe a small group setting helps people get to know each other and make meaningful connections.

FAQ


What if I'm not a design intern but I like design?
Design Summer is for you! We celebrate diversity of thought and want people to connect over the different kinds of work they do.

What if I'm not a current intern?
Because space at events is limited, only current interns can participate. Sorry!
What am I signing up for?
We plan to organize meals, meetups, workshops, and excursions to companies and museums in the Bay Area to build a community around design. You're signing up to be notified of when these events occur so that you can register for them.

Got more questions? Email us at info@designsummer.me.
