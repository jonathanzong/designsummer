About


The purpose of Design Summer is to organize events for Bay Area interns interested in design. Design has become more and more prevalent in an increasing number of industries. Over the course of a summer, we plan to organize lunches, tours, workshops, and expeditions around the Bay Area in order to allow us to give those interested the opportunity to interact with industry leading designers and gain exposure to the thriving design scene in San Francisco and its surroundings.


One summer. Infinite possibilities.
